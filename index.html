Veecle is becoming infrastructure for AI agents.

We are rebuilding Veecle around the Model Context Protocol (MCP) — an open standard that lets AI coding agents like Claude and Cursor interact directly with tools, environments, and hardware targets. Instead of a browser IDE you log into, Veecle will expose virtual chips and embedded execution environments as MCP servers: composable, agent-native infrastructure that plugs into the AI tools your team already uses.

Studio is paused while we ship this. Follow our progress at veecle.ai.
